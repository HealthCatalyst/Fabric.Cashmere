Overview

Health Catalyst strives to use language and abbreviations consistently across corporate, marketing, education, and product documentation. This guide defines the house style for Health Catalyst.

For style decisions not defined in this guide, follow the American Psychological Association Style Guide (for general word style) or the Microsoft Writing Style Guide (for UX/technical writing).
