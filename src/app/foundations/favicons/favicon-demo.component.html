Favicon Guidelines

Last updated May 17, 2023
Overview

Favicons are the small icons displayed in the browser's address bar next to the page's title. They're also used for bookmarks. We have a standard look and feel for favicons using Cashmere $blue overlayed with either the app's initials or the Health Catalyst triflame.

The Health Catalyst design team has a template to create these, so you can request one from them in #design on slack.
Setup Instructions
  1. Obtain a 260x260 pixel png favicon from the design team.
  2. Go to Real Favicon Generator and upload your favicon. We'll use this tool to generate all the needed assets.
  3. After confirming the image, you'll be presented with configuration. We'll leave things as is except for the output path.
    1. Scroll down to the bottom of the configuration page to change the path. Favicon Generator Options > Path.
    2. Select "I cannot or do not want to place favicon files at the root..."
    3. Create a new folder in your codebase under to house the files the generator will create. Something like src/assets/favico.
    4. Enter the path to your newly created favico folder:
  4. Click the Generate button.
  5. Download the generated .zip file and extract it into the folder referenced above. We do this to avoid muddying up the root directory.
    • As a fallback, also add a copy of favico.ico to the root of your app (right next to index.html).
  6. Grab the generated HTML and stick it in the <head> of your index.html file.
  7. [Optional] Consider linking to this process in your readme in case future updates are needed.
