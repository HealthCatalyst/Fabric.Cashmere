Overview

Health Catalyst's products are not sub-brands themselves. The house brand is the central and most important market identifier for all of our software applications. But within the house brand, apps still need a way to be clearly distinguished from one another. The product icon, along with the Flexo font version of its name (used in the navbar) are the two unique visual identifiers for an app. They are to be used in all promotional or training materials related to the app, as well as any websites that reference the app (Health Catalyst Community, Docs, etc).

Click any of the icons below to download a zip containing an SVG and PNG of that icon. Or use the purple button to download the entire collection.
