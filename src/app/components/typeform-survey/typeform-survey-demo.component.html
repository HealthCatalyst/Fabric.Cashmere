About

Typeform Survey component is a wrapper to the typeform survey embed script.

Example
Component Usage

Any survey created in Typeform may be linked using this component. For Health Catalyst app developers, please contact the Customer Feedback team for the correct survey to link to within your app. The typeform survey component should be used in conjunction with application specific logic that prompts the user to take a survey at predetermined intervals. See the User Feedback Guide for more information and best practices.
