Font Guidelines

Last updated July 12, 2023
Display Typefaces

Archivo

Our brand display typeface is Archivo. This typeface is replacing Flexo, our legacy display font, but does not affect the logo. The display typeface should be applied only to headlines,sub-headlines, and accent text, and only the core font family should be used. The core font family is available in seven weights, but only Bold and Medium are approved for use within our brand. Do not use italics.

Download Archivo

Open Sans

Our secondary typeface - including all weights and variants.

Download Open Sans
Secondary Typefaces

Noto Serif

A secondary body copy font that may be used in large bodies of text, but should always support headlines and other elements in the primary brand font, Archivo.

Download Noto Serif

Noto Sans

Has expanded glyph and non-Latin character sets, and should be used in web, development, and international materials. Note: the Cashmere library and this site use Noto Sans as their base font. Noto Sans is very similar to Open Sans visually, but supports all international character sets. Additional information about Google's open source Noto font family can be found at their documentation site.

Download Noto Sans

Calibri

May be used for internal documents and presentations when core primary and secondary typefaces are unavailable.
