Healthcare.AI™ Branding

Last updated March 3, 2023
Overview

Heathcare.AI™ is HCAT's AI framework that expands the use and use cases for effective AI for our customers.

In order to call out and tie together the uses of AI in our products, we recommend using standard visual branding. When someone sees this branding we want them to think "Oh! I know what this is, and it's going to be really useful. I love Healthcare.AI." There are two formats for this branding:

Standard

When space allows, this is the preferred format.

Badge

For cases with limited space, use this secondary style. Also include the preferred 'Standard' format elsewhere on the page.
