About

Drawers are great for content that you want tucked away, like filters. They cover the entire height of their parent container, and slide in from either left or right.

Examples

You can have both a left and right drawer

Left drawer content Right drawer content

You can have the drawer push or overlay your content

Left drawer content Right drawer content

Angular Component
            
<hc-drawer-container>
<hc-drawer>
    Drawer Content
</hc-drawer>
Main Content
</hc-drawer-container>
Component Parameters
mode Type: 'over' | 'push' (default: 'push') Optional - overlay or push content
align Type: 'left' | 'right' (default: 'left') Optional - alignment of the drawer.
opened Type: Boolean (default: false) Optional - whether the drawer starts out opened/closed
*Note: An error will be thrown if there are more than two drawers in hc-drawer-container or if both drawers have the same alignment
